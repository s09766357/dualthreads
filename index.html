1. Purpose

DualThreads (the “App”) uses the Meta Threads API to support automated posting, reply management, and insights retrieval for the operator’s Threads account(s). This Privacy Policy explains how data is collected, used, stored, and protected by the App.

2. Data We Collect and Handle

  • Content Data: post text, reply text, content IDs, parent/child relation IDs, timestamps.
  • Insights / Metrics: impressions, likes, replies, reposts, quotes, and other analytics available via the Threads API.
  • Technical Data: OAuth access/refresh tokens, execution and error logs, IP address, and similar data processed for security and operation.
  • Operational Data: posting schedules, drafts, and data stored in connected tools (e.g., Google Sheets) for operation of the App.

3. How We Use the Data

  • Create and manage posts and replies on Threads (including automation).
  • Retrieve and analyze insights for operational reporting and performance measurement.
  • Improve service quality, troubleshoot issues, ensure security, and comply with applicable laws and platform policies.

4. Retention

We retain data only for as long as necessary to fulfill the purposes above. By default, we keep data for up to 12 months, unless a longer period is required by law or legitimate record-keeping obligations. Data that is no longer needed will be deleted without undue delay.

5. Sharing and Disclosure

We do not sell or share personal data with third parties, except with trusted processors necessary to operate the App (e.g., Google Apps Script / Google Sheets, hosting providers). Such processors handle data under appropriate confidentiality and security obligations. We may also disclose information if required by law or to protect rights, safety, or security.

6. Security

  • Access tokens are stored in secure storage under access control (e.g., Google Apps Script Properties).
  • Principle of least privilege, rate limiting, and audit of access/activity logs.
  • Transport security via HTTPS and timely remediation of known vulnerabilities in dependencies.

7. Your Rights

You may request access, correction, restriction, or deletion of data that the App controls. Please see our Data Deletion page for the procedure. We will respond within a reasonable time and in accordance with applicable law and platform rules.

8. Children

The App is not intended for use by children under the age of 13. If we learn that such data has been inadvertently processed, we will delete it promptly.

9. Changes to This Policy

We may update this Policy from time to time. When we do, we will revise the “Last updated” date above. Material changes will be communicated by reasonable means.
