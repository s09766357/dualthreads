Data Deletion Instructions

App: DualThreads
Contact: affi_tanmatsu_52@icloud.com
Last updated: October 15, 2025

1. Scope of Deletion

These instructions cover data the App controls in relation to Meta Threads, including post/reply IDs and text, timestamps, insights/metrics, OAuth tokens, operational logs, and operational data stored in connected tools (e.g., Google Sheets).

2. How to Request Deletion

  1. Send an email to affi_tanmatsu_52@icloud.com with the subject “Data Deletion Request”.
  2. Include:
    • Your Threads username or user ID;
    • The scope of deletion (e.g., all data, a date range, specific posts);
    • A reply-to contact for verification purposes.
  3. After verifying your identity and request details, we will begin deletion.

3. Timeline and Confirmation

We will delete the relevant data from our storage locations (e.g., Google Apps Script Properties, Google Sheets, operational logs) and send a confirmation email, typically within five business days of verification.

4. Requests from Meta (Threads)

We also act on data deletion or de-authorization requests received via Meta/Threads mechanisms, and will delete relevant data promptly upon such notifications.

5. Revoking Access

You may revoke the App’s access in your Meta account settings at any time. You can still request deletion of data we control via the steps above.

6. Questions

If you have any questions, please contact affi_tanmatsu_52@icloud.com.
